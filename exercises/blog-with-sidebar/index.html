ADULTERERS OR FRIENDS? Naruto and Sasuke CAUGHT on a DATE at INFAMOUS Romantic Diner the day of Himawari's BIRTHDAY!

by Ada Wong

·

June 20th 2025

Sources say that the Hokage sent a shadow clone to his daughter's birthday party to spend some personal quality time with his "best friend". How nefarious.

It was a sunny afternoon in Konoha, and the village was abuzz with excitement—it was Himawari Uzumaki’s birthday. Balloons floated, confetti flew, and Iruka-sensei was somehow in charge of the bouncy castle logistics.

But where was Naruto? Not at the party.

Because the Seventh Hokage had just executed the most outrageous Shadow Clone Jutsu scam of his entire morally-questionable dad career.

When A Girl Doesn't Mind Her Business

Somewhere a few rooftops over, Tenten limped down the street, muttering curses under her breath and clutching a bag of ice to her cheek. Why? Because earlier that day, she’d decided it was a great idea to ask Temari if she was "still just Gaara’s sister or finally Shikamaru’s wife-slash-problem." Temari smiled. Then she hit her with a fan gust so hard, Tenten landed in the weapons shop window and still had time to regret her entire bloodline.

As she hobbled past Ichiraku’s Rooftop Ramen Hideout, something caught her eye.

Naruto.
And Sasuke.
Sitting way too close.
Sharing mochi like this was a romantic filler episode.
Tenten squinted. “…Isn’t today Himawari’s birthday?” She looked harder. Naruto fed Sasuke a bite of ramen. Sasuke didn’t stab him. Confirmed: Scandal. Tenten immediately took out her phone and snapped a picture with the flash on by accident. Naruto turned in horror. Tenten, holding the ice pack and bleeding a little, just yelled from the alleyway: "YOU’RE GOING TO HELL, HOKAGE SCAMMER. TELL HINATA I’M ON HER SIDE." Then she slipped on a takoyaki tray and disappeared into a dumpster.
